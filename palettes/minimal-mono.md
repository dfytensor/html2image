# Minimal Mono Palette

For quotes, typography, editorial, premium minimal content.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | White | `#FFFFFF` | Page background |
| Text | Near-black | `#111111` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Dark gray | `#333333` | Highlights, emphasis |
| Cards | Border-only | `#111111 1px solid` | Content cards |
| Muted | Mid gray | `#999999` | Secondary text |
| Surface | Light gray | `#F5F5F5` | Subtle backgrounds |

## Dark Variant

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Black | `#000000` |
| Text | White | `#FFFFFF` |
| Accent | Light gray | `#CCCCCC` |
| Muted | Dark gray | `#666666` |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Warm black | `#1A1614` | Warm mono |
| Cool black | `#141618` | Cool mono |
| Pure red | `#FF0000` | Single-color accent pop |
| Navy | `#000080` | Intellectual mono |

## Decorative Elements

- Zero or minimal border radius (0-4px)
- Hairline borders (1px)
- Generous white space (40%+ empty)
- Single accent line or dot
- No gradients (flat solid only)
- Serif fonts strongly preferred
