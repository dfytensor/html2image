# Dark Aesthetic Palette

For luxury, mood, premium, night, photography, cinematic content.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Near-black | `#0F0F0F` | Page background |
| Text | Off-white | `#F5F5F5` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Crimson | `#E94560` | Highlights, numbers |
| Cards | Dark card | `#1E1E1E` | Content cards |
| Muted | Warm gray | `#888888` | Secondary text |
| Surface | Dark navy | `#1A1A2E` | Secondary background |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Gold | `#FFC300` | Luxury, premium |
| Rose gold | `#B76E79` | Elegant warmth |
| Ice blue | `#66FCF1` | Tech/futuristic |
| Deep purple | `#6C3483` | Mystery |

## Gradient Presets

```css
background: linear-gradient(180deg, #0F0F0F 0%, #1A1A2E 100%);
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #E94560 0%, #FFC300 100%);
background: radial-gradient(ellipse at 50% 30%, rgba(233,69,96,0.15) 0%, transparent 50%);
```

## Decorative Elements

- Subtle rounded corners (8-12px)
- Border glow effects (1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.06))
- Grain/noise texture overlay
- Minimal gradient blobs
- Thin hairline rules
- Serif fonts for titles
