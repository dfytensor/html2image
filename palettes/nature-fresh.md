# Nature Fresh Palette

For outdoors, sustainability, health, gardening, organic content.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Mint white | `#F0FFF4` | Page background |
| Text | Forest green | `#1B4332` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Green | `#2D6A4F` | Highlights, numbers |
| Cards | White | `#FFFFFF` | Content cards |
| Muted | Sage gray | `#6B7C6E` | Secondary text |
| Surface | Light sage | `#D8F3DC` | Secondary background |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Earth brown | `#8B6914` | Grounding accent |
| Sky blue | `#87CEEB` | Fresh highlights |
| Sunflower | `#F4D35E` | Warm pop |
| Sage | `#95D5B2` | Soft emphasis |

## Gradient Presets

```css
background: linear-gradient(180deg, #F0FFF4 0%, #D8F3DC 100%);
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #2D6A4F 0%, #95D5B2 100%);
background: radial-gradient(ellipse at 10% 90%, #D8F3DC 0%, transparent 50%);
```

## Decorative Elements

- Organic rounded corners (16-24px)
- Leaf and branch line art
- Botanical SVG motifs
- Curved wave dividers
- Earthy texture overlays
