# Warm Lifestyle Palette

For food, wellness, home, lifestyle, personal growth content.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Warm cream | `#FFF8F0` | Page background |
| Text | Dark brown | `#2D2016` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Warm orange | `#E8833A` | Highlights, numbers, icons |
| Cards | White | `#FFFFFF` | Content cards |
| Muted | Warm gray | `#8B7355` | Secondary text, captions |
| Surface | Light peach | `#FFE8D6` | Secondary background, hover |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Deep orange | `#D4721A` | Dark emphasis |
| Light orange | `#F4A261` | Soft highlights |
| Peach | `#FFDAB9` | Background accents |
| Amber | `#FFBF00` | Warning/highlight badges |

## Gradient Presets

```css
/* Warm cream gradient */
background: linear-gradient(180deg, #FFF8F0 0%, #FFE8D6 100%);

/* Warm accent gradient */
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #E8833A 0%, #F4A261 100%);

/* Soft warm blob */
background: radial-gradient(ellipse at 80% 20%, #FFE8D6 0%, transparent 60%);
```

## Typography Pairing

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Title | System sans-serif (rounded) | 800 |
| Body | System sans-serif | 400 |
| Accent | Serif italic | 400 |

## Decorative Elements

- Rounded corners (16-24px) on cards
- Soft drop shadows (0 2px 12px rgba(0,0,0,0.06))
- Wavy dividers between sections
- Warm-toned blob shapes in corners
- Small leaf or heart line-art motifs
