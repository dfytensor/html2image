# Bold Social Palette

For fitness, motivation, bold statements, challenges, transformation.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Dark navy | `#1A1A2E` | Page background |
| Text | White | `#FFFFFF` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Bright yellow | `#FFD23F` | Highlights, numbers |
| Cards | Semi-transparent | `rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` | Content cards |
| Muted | Cool gray | `#AAAAAA` | Secondary text |
| Surface | Vibrant orange | `#FF6B35` | Feature backgrounds |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Electric blue | `#4FACFE` | Cool contrast |
| Hot pink | `#FF0080` | Energy pop |
| Lime | `#BFFF00` | Fresh highlights |
| Coral | `#FF6B6B` | Warning/important |

## Gradient Presets

```css
background: linear-gradient(180deg, #1A1A2E 0%, #16213E 100%);
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #FF6B35 0%, #FFD23F 100%);
background: radial-gradient(ellipse at 50% 0%, #FF6B35 0%, transparent 50%);
```

## Decorative Elements

- Sharp corners or minimal radius (4-8px)
- Bold borders (2-4px solid accent)
- Arrow and chevron motifs
- Diagonal stripe accents
- Large number typography (number is the hero)
