# Clean Knowledge Palette

For education, science, tech tips, tutorials, explainers.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Cool gray | `#F8F9FA` | Page background |
| Text | Dark navy | `#1A1A2E` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Blue | `#0984E3` | Highlights, numbers |
| Cards | White | `#FFFFFF` | Content cards |
| Muted | Steel gray | `#6C7A89` | Secondary text |
| Surface | Light blue | `#E8F4FD` | Info callouts |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Deep blue | `#0652DD` | Dark emphasis |
| Teal | `#00B894` | Success/positive |
| Light blue | `#74B9FF` | Soft highlights |
| Indigo | `#5F27CD` | Premium feel |

## Gradient Presets

```css
background: linear-gradient(180deg, #F8F9FA 0%, #E8F4FD 100%);
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #0984E3 0%, #74B9FF 100%);
background: radial-gradient(ellipse at 90% 10%, #E8F4FD 0%, transparent 40%);
```

## Decorative Elements

- Clean corners (12-16px)
- Subtle shadows (0 1px 4px rgba(0,0,0,0.08))
- Geometric lines and dots
- Grid pattern backgrounds
- Data-style accents (rulers, measurement lines)
