# Pastel Soft Palette

For beauty, fashion, baby, gentle topics, romance.

---

## Primary Palette

| Role | Color | Hex | Usage |
|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Background | Soft pink | `#FDF2F8` | Page background |
| Text | Dark plum | `#4A3040` | Body text, titles |
| Accent | Pink | `#F472B6` | Highlights, numbers |
| Cards | White | `#FFFFFF` | Content cards |
| Muted | Cool gray | `#9CA3AF` | Secondary text |
| Surface | Lavender tint | `#F3E8FF` | Secondary background |

## Accent Variants

| Name | Hex | Use When |
|------|-----|----------|
| Rose | `#FB7185` | Strong emphasis |
| Lavender | `#A78BFA` | Alternative accent |
| Soft blue | `#93C5FD` | Calm highlights |
| Peach | `#FBBF24` | Warm pop |

## Gradient Presets

```css
background: linear-gradient(180deg, #FDF2F8 0%, #F3E8FF 100%);
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #F472B6 0%, #A78BFA 100%);
background: radial-gradient(ellipse at 20% 80%, #F3E8FF 0%, transparent 50%);
```

## Decorative Elements

- Extra rounded corners (20-28px)
- Very soft shadows (0 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.04))
- Flower/star scatter motifs
- Dotted borders on cards
- Curved section dividers
